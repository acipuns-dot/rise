## ü•ó Nutrition Roadmap (Adjusting as you go)
*   **Weeks 1-4:** Focus on **120g Protein** and **3L Water**. Don't worry about total calories yet.
*   **Weeks 5-8:** Keep protein high. Start reducing "Liquid Calories" (sodas/juices) to accelerate thigh leaning.
*   **Weeks 9-12:** Maintenance calories. High protein, high water. This is where you'll see the 73kg look completely different in the mirror.
